Dice Drinking Game

How to play:

  1. Indicate how many pieces of dice to play with.
  2. Choose a number from 1-6. Then roll the dice.
    • A) If the number you've chosen appears in the set, any dice that reflects that number will be removed. You don't need to drink. Pass to the next person.
    • B) If the number you've chosen doesn't appear in the set, you need to drink. You cannot pass to the next person until you guess a number correctly.
  4. Play until there's no more dice left.
